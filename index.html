BISHAMONTEN

THE POWER OF DRAGON

Bishamonten (毘び沙しゃ門もん天てん, Bishamonten ) also known as Vaisravana or Bishamonten, is one of the Seven Lucky Gods. She is officially the goddess of war and warriors. They believe that taking taking control of their lives is the first step to making change for the struggle. But to do that, they need to understand how money work, and it is not taught at home or at school.

This is where Bishamonten $BISH comes in, providing content who teaches them how money works when they invest, opens up opportunities for wealth creation. Our goal is to fostering human relations through filling financial literacy this gap in the crypto and financial markets and prepare the next generation of investors.
